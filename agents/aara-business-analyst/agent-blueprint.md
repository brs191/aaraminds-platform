## Business Problem

Requirements work on client engagements is hand-assembled: elicitation notes, gap analyses, and acceptance criteria are produced ad hoc with no shared structure, no traceability from business objective to requirement, and no evidence discipline. Each engagement restarts from scratch.

- Every requirement traceable to an engagement source or marked as assumption
- Acceptance criteria generated alongside requirements, not after
