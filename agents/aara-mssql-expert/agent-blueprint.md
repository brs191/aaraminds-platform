## Business Problem

Teams write, review, and optimize T-SQL stored procedures, functions, and queries for Azure SQL Database and Managed Instance by hand, with uneven expertise and frequent SQL-Server-specific defects: parameter-sniffing plan regressions, NOLOCK misuse, injection-prone dynamic SQL, and incorrect isolation assumptions. Schema knowledge is often assumed rather than verified, producing subtly wrong T-SQL that fails only under production load.
